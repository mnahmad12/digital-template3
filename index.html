Collect the Cats!

  • High Concept: Collect the cats and drop them in the box, Before the time runs out!
  • Player Experience Goals: A race against the clock!
  • Genre: Simple kids game
  • Game play: This is a simple game, push the cats into the box before time runs out!
  • Prototype goal: 1) Movement 2) Collisions
  • Features: The pushing of the cats makes it a little bit harder as the physics of movement will be a little bit difficult to get!
  • Setting/Premise: None
  • Story: None
  • Target Audience: Kids who are easily distracted by simple games
  • Play time: Currently the game is only one level, so around 30 seconds-1 minute.
  • Strategies: Find all of the cats and push them in as soon as possible. Try to find the furthest ones first.
  • Prototype Assets: Internet, google images.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: None really.
